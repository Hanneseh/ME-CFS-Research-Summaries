This index tracks research directions focused on how ME/CFS and related post-viral illness are measured, characterized, and cared for in clinical and research settings.

## Active Diagnostic, Symptom & Care Threads

- **[Post-Exertional Malaise & Exercise Physiology](./pem-exercise-physiology/)** - PEM, exertion response, CPET, lactate recovery, pacing physiology, actigraphy, and exercise testing.
- **[Diagnostics & Biomarkers](./diagnostics-biomarkers/)** - diagnostic tests, biomarker panels, machine-learning classifiers, biosensors, assays, and measurement tools.
- **[Clinical Characterization & Epidemiology](./clinical-characterization-epidemiology/)** - case definitions, clinical guidelines, epidemiology, cohort structure, symptom scales, project/funding announcements, ethics, and trial-design context.
- **[Care & Assessment for Severe ME-CFS](./severe-mecfs-care/)** - severe and very severe ME/CFS care, home visits, outreach diagnostics, anesthesia/perioperative safety, and bedbound patient assessment.
- **[Small Fiber Neuropathy](./small-fiber-neuropathy/)** - peripheral small-fiber neuropathy, dysautonomia overlap, Sudoscan, evoked potentials, and mast-cell or neuroimmune mechanisms.

## How To Read These Threads

Diagnostic and care-context threads separate measurement validity, patient characterization, and clinical-support evidence from treatment claims. A biomarker, symptom scale, or exercise protocol can be useful for research before it is ready for routine clinical decision-making.
