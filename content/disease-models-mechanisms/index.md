This index tracks research directions that try to explain what may be going wrong biologically. These threads are organized around disease models and mechanisms rather than treatment names.

## Active Mechanism Threads

- **[Viral Persistence & Reactivation](./viral-persistence-reactivation/)** - pathogen reservoirs, EBV and herpesvirus reactivation, viral proteins, and post-viral triggers.
- **[Immune Dysregulation & Inflammation](./immune-dysregulation-inflammation/)** - NK cells, T cells, B cells, cytokines, monocytes, mast cells, and inflammatory pathways.
- **[Autoimmunity & Autoantibodies](./autoimmunity-autoantibodies/)** - GPCR autoantibodies, receptor targeting, antigen profiling, and IgG-mediated mechanisms.
- **[Mitochondrial & Metabolic Dysfunction](./mitochondrial-metabolic-dysfunction/)** - cellular bioenergetics, metabolomics, lipidomics, and skeletal muscle energy failure.
- **[Vascular Endothelial Dysfunction & Microclots](./vascular-endothelial-microclots/)** - endothelial impairment, microclots, RBC deformability, hypercoagulability, and perfusion pathology.
- **[Autonomic Dysfunction & POTS](./autonomic-dysfunction-pots/)** - orthostatic intolerance, HRV, baroreflex control, dysautonomia, and autonomic nervous-system dysfunction.
- **[Neuroinflammation & Brain Changes](./neuroinflammation-brain-changes/)** - brain imaging, CNS inflammation, microglia, neurovascular coupling, and cognitive dysfunction mechanisms.
- **[Gut Microbiome & Dysbiosis](./gut-microbiome-dysbiosis/)** - microbiome, mycobiome, gut barrier dysfunction, bacterial translocation, and gut-derived signals.
- **[Genetic Risk Factors](./genetic-risk-factors/)** - GWAS, WGS, DecodeME-style association studies, Mendelian randomization, and genetic risk profiles.

## How To Read These Threads

Mechanism threads are explanatory maps. A mechanism can be plausible and useful without being proven as the cause of ME/CFS, so each thread should separate established observations, plausible models, and open questions.
